Campus Ambassador Program

The Campus Ambassador Program at FnPlus community is aimed at identifying energetic, passionate, brilliant, like minded College Superheroes who have the zeal to take initiative and build an engaging community.

Apply Now

How to Identify a Hero?

Well, they are the people who are typically great coders and very active members of Computer societies, clubs, events and activities. In addition, they are stars of their community and people look upto them since they usually lead by example. In short, Leadership flows in their veins and they are the visionaries of tomorrow.


If you feel you have the qualities to be a Superhero, we invite you to join the clan and be a part of the community. We assure you of a great learning experience, awesome perks and take home rewards, a super confident you, a shining resume with recommendations & certificate and a place in the #fnplus family.

How Do I Become a Hero?

Roles and Responsibilities of Superheroes

  • Be the official Super Hero alias Ambassador and branding representative of FnPlus in your college
  • Publicise by displaying posters on notice boards, sharing e-posters on WhatsApp, Facebook etc, circulating emails, etc.
  • Maintain relevant databases and submit monthly reports
  • Conduct minimum of FnPlus workshops every 2 months and programming events / online hackathon every 45 days in your campus
  • Organize counselling sessions and meet-ups in college canteen/campus with Mentors & Alumni
  • Represent us in the placement cells of your college
  • Come up with innovative ideas on how Fnplus can assist your college and associate us with any technical fest or sessions that your college conducts
  • Get students to attend difficult subject doubt session ‘Webinars’ – organised by Mentors of FnPlus
  • Associate FnPlus with various college societies and fest department

What You Learn & Earn

  • Superhero kit with host of swags like T-shirt, Bag, Cap, Diary, Pen drive, Stickers and so on.
  • Work Experience Certificate to all eligible candidates on successful completion of six months program
  • LinkedIn recommendation to all eligible candidates after completion of the program
  • Free passes for workshops
  • Opportunity to interact and network with industry experts and professionals through various events
  • Opportunity to become a part of us (PPO)
  • Huge cash incentives for specific target achievements
  • Exposure of working with a start-up, with some of the best brains in the industry
  • Campus ambassadors are preferred for Paid internships and Teaching Assistants
  • Acclamation of efforts through Superheros of the Month, Quarter and Year entitling you to Cask rewards, Mobile phones respectively.
